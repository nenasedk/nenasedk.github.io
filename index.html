Research Interests

I'm mostly interested in exoplanet observation and characterization. My current project is characterizing how instrumental effects in the MIRI MRS will impact atmospheric retrievals of brown dwarfs and Jupiter-like planets. In the long term I want to work towards measuring biosignatures in the atmospheres of terrestrial planets to determine prospects for habitability: are we alone out here?

Apart from astronomy, I'm an avid musician and photographer, and love travelling around the world to learn about new cultures and people.
